## About Me

I am a systems-oriented backend engineer trained at **42 Brussels (Campus 19)**.  
My engineering background started with **C and C++**, giving me strong foundations in low-level programming, memory safety, networking, and performance-critical development.

Over the years, I specialized in **Rust** and **Golang**, building:

- low-latency backend services  
- async systems (Tokio)  
- market-data ingestion pipelines  
- algorithmic trading tools  
- high-performance APIs

I am fluent in French, Dutch and English, and I am especially interested in **finance**, **trading systems**, **backend performance**, and **secure system design**.
